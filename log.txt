In the foreign exchange market, special factors of influence on the ruble exchange rate appear.Their influence on the dollar may be unexpected.What will happen to the exchange rate next week?
Economists said what will happen to the ruble course next week.Cover © TASS / Sergey Konkov
In the coming week, currency courses will be determined not so much by market events as political.So, in the UK and France, parliamentary elections are held.The right block led by Marin Le Pen shows serious results.However, the exact composition of the parliament will be known only after the second round of elections.Perurbation in the EU zone weakens the euro.In the UK, the question remains open, how overwhelming the majority of the Labor Party will be and whether it will be enough.Thus, Oleg Kalmanovich, chief analyst at Neomarkets, described the atmosphere in the markets.
- On Friday, we expect another publication of several important reports at once on Friday, for example, Nonfarm Peirols.Data on the labor market in the United States will also be updated.These values ​​will largely affect the position of the dollar in the world market in the coming week, ”added Oleg Kalmanovich.
From the events of the next week important for the foreign exchange market, it is possible to highlight the publication of data on July 11 on the growth rate of consumer prices in the USA (IPC).If the indicator turns out to be much higher or lower than the expectations, you can wait for the violent reaction of the global dollar (DXY index).This usually affects the quotes important for the ruble of energy and metals.At such a moment, the investment strategist "BCS Investment World" Alexander Bakhtin drew attention.
Despite the over-the-way regulation of currencies, world exchange quotes are still affected by the courses.Within a week, a gradual strengthening of the ruble is expected to a mark of 86.2 per dollar.Such a forecast was done by Oleg Kalmanovich.
- The official ruble courses for the dollar and Yuan got up to 88 and 12.2, respectively.The euro "looks" at the mark 94 and a little higher.Next week, we expect a decrease in the course volatility with a gradual shift in the yuan to 12.4, the euro in zone 94–95, the dollar - 88–89 rubles.High rates in the economy, as well as temporary difficulties with cross -border calculations for delivered imports that arose against the background of sanctions pressure, ”said Alexander Bakhtin, restraining the devaluation.
In his opinion, importers in the coming weeks will reconfigure the main schemes of calculations.As a result, the demand for currency will gradually revive.
- Today, many factors are affected by currencies today.Some of them can have unpredictable effects.The key prices are oil prices, the amounts of taxes from exporters, as well as a total foreign trade balance and monetary policy within the country.Reducing prices for hydrocarbons can lead to weakening the ruble, and growth - to its strengthening.Do not also forget about the impact of sanctions.In my opinion, in the near future we can see quotes in the next corridor: up to 90 rubles per dollar, about 95–96 rubles per euro and about 12.2 rubles for Yuan, ”said the private investor, founder of the school of practical investment Fedor Sidorov.
On Wednesday, the ruble continued to decline in the off -off market.However, compared with the previous session, the pace of its decrease was reduced.The return movement ends, the market comes into balance.The US dollar is growing to 88.33 rubles (+0.56%).The euro is strengthened by 1.54% to 94.91.The volumes of trading look medium.
-Most likely, another day or two-and the dollar with the euro is stabilized at the Over-time venue.If the situation remains the same, then the dollar will balance within 88–89 rubles, the euro-around 95 rubles, ”said the senior trader of the Alfa Capital Criminal Code Vladislav Silaev.
The main events are expected in the markets in the last week of July.Then the Central Bank will decide at the key rate.Now the course will stake on current values ​​in anticipation of the end of the month.Such a forecast was made by the financial market expert Nikolai Salabuto.
- Next week, the ruble may be influenced by the Ministry of Finance of purchases of currency by the budget rule.It will grow 1.5 times compared to June.This can affect the ruble negatively.At the same time, the factors restraining the fall of the ruble may be mandatory sale of foreign exchange earnings and not too high population demand for currency, since interest rates on ruble deposits are already very high.High oil prices can also provide ruble support.We expect a dollar in the corridor of 87–90 rubles, and the euro - 93–96, is an economist, Bitriver communications director Andrei Loboda.
According to the results of the first half of the year, the Russian currency was strengthened against the dollar, the euro and the yuan by 5.8%, 7.7% and 7.4%, respectively.The new boundaries of the ruble strengthening 79 per dollar, 85 per euro and 10.85 for Yuan completely played a fundamental positive (tax period and the lack of demand for foreign currency from importers).Now the market needs time to secure the course in a new offset.The oscillations of the dollar are expected within 83–89 rubles, the euro 89–95 rubles, the yuan is 11.40–12.40 rubles.This opinion was expressed by the head of the research strategies of Alfa-Fordex Spartak Sobolev.
